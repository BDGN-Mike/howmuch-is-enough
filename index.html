A Question Worth Asking

Most of us spend our lives pursuing more.

More income. More possessions. More security. More success.

But eventually, a different question begins to emerge:

How much is enough?

For some, the question comes when planning retirement. For others, it comes after years of hard work, career success, or major life changes.

At some point, many of us begin to wonder:

  • How much money do I really need?
  • How much should I save for retirement?
  • How much is enough to live comfortably?
  • How much is enough to be happy?
  • How much is enough to live a meaningful life?

This website was created to explore those questions.

Through personal stories, financial insights, retirement planning, lifestyle discussions, and interviews with people from different walks of life, we'll examine what "enough" means—and how finding your own answer can change everything.

Whether you're approaching retirement, considering a move abroad, pursuing financial independence, or simply seeking a more balanced life, we invite you to join the conversation.

Why This Website Exists

After decades of building businesses, investing, traveling, and helping people connect across cultures, I found myself asking a surprisingly difficult question:

How much is enough?

Enough money. Enough success. Enough possessions. Enough security.

The more people I spoke with, the more I realized that everyone defines "enough" differently.

Some discover it while preparing for retirement. Others discover it after achieving financial success. Some discover it through travel, family, health challenges, or simply growing older and gaining perspective.

This website is an exploration of that question.

Together we'll examine what enough means financially, emotionally, physically, and spiritually—and why finding your own answer may be one of life's most important journeys.

— Mike Krosky
